Characteristics of Metals

  • Tend to be solid with high boiling and melting points and relatively high densities.
  • But as with many properties, there are exceptions; for example, mercury is liquid.
  • Shiny
  • Malleable
  • Ductile
  • Good conductors of electricity and heat
  • Form ionic bonds
  • Form positive ions in their compounds
