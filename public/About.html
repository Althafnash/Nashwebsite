Nash SecondBrain

A Second Brain is a personal knowledge management system designed to help you capture, organize, and retrieve ideas, information, and insights for better productivity and creativity. Acting as an external, digital repository, it enables you to offload mental clutter and store everything from notes, project plans, and research to inspirations and reflections. Tools like Notion, Obsidian, Roam Research, or Evernote are commonly used to build a Second Brain, which often integrates frameworks like Tiago Forte's PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) to streamline categorization and accessibility.
