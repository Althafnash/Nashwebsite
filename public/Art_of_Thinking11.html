Survivorship Bias

In daily life, because triumph is made more visible than failure, you systematically overestimate your chances of succeeding.

As an outsider, you succumb to an illusion and mistake how minuscule the probability of success really is.

Survivorship bias means that people systematically overestimate their chances of success.

Guard against it by frequently visiting the graves of one's promising projects, investments, and careers.
