Newton's Three Laws of Force

Newton's First Law

Until an unbalanced force is applied, bodies in motion will continue to move, and bodies at rest will remain at rest.

Newton's Second Law

The acceleration of an object is directly proportional to the unbalanced force and inversely proportional to its mass.

  • A is directly proportional to F
  • A is directly proportional to 1 / M
  • F / M / A = constant
  • F = M * A

Newton's Third Law

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.
