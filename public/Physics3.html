Friction

The force that acts against the relative motion is known as friction

There are three types of friction they are

  • static friction
  • limiting friction
  • dynamic friction

The frictional force which acts on the body before the motion starts is known as static frictional force

The maximum frictional force between the surfaces of two bodies in contact with one another is known as limiting friction

The frictional force acting on the body after the body starts to move is known as dynamic friction

The limiting frictional force depends on the nature of the two contact surfaces and the normal reaction between the two surfaces

Methods of reducing friction

  • reducing the roughness of the contact surfaces
  • applying lubricants between the two surfaces
  • inserting balls that could roll between the contact surfaces
